Initial:

  1. Max length of a snake is 10 (max health is 100)
  2. Initial length is 1
  3. Number of foods is (number of players - 1)

Attack:

Every time a snake is hit by head of another snake its length will be reduced by 1 a and health reduced by 10 respectively.

Edge cases:

  1. "head-to-head": when two snakes hit on each other with their heads, their healths should both reduce by 10 (length reduced by 1 respectively)
  2. “hit-on-wall”: when a snake hits a wall, the snake will reduce health by 10 (length by 1) for each time step until user change the direction.

Food-health model:

The total number of food in the board should be (number of users - 1) so as to force competition. Once a food is taken another one will be generated randomly into the board. Once a snake takes in a food the length will be increased by 1 (health increased by 10 until it reaches the maximum health).
