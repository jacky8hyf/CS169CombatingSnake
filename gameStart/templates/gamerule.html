Initial Health and Damage:

Each snake has its health initially set to 10 hp.

Attack Model:

Every time a snake is hit by head of another snake, its health will be reduced by 10 (length reduced by 1 respectively).

Food-health Model:

The total number of food in the board should be (number of users - 1) so as to force competition. Once a food is taken another one will be generated randomly into the board. Once a snake takes in a food the length will be increased by 1 (health increased by 10 until it reaches the maximum health) to win.
