Health:

Each snake has its health initially set to 10 hp and its length set to 1.

Attack:

Every time a snake is hit by head of another snake, its length will be reduced by 1 (health reduced by 10 respectively).

Food:

The total number of food in the board should be (number of users - 1) so as to force competition. Once a food is taken another one will be generated randomly into the board. Once a snake takes in a food the length will be increased by 1 (health increased by 10 until it reaches the maximum health) to win.

Wall:

Every time the snake hits the wall, its length will decrease by 1.

Win:

  • The snake wins when its length reaches 10
  • The snake also wins when all the other snakes have died.
